# About Ismail

Ismail Demir has a strong background in computer science and applied artificial intelligence, with a particular focus on machine learning and medical AI.

He has completed advanced-level coursework in Artificial Intelligence at the Technical University of Munich (TUM), where he studied topics such as Machine Learning, AI in Medicine, Data Mining, and Multimodal AI. In addition to his studies, he participated in a research project within the Chair for AI in Medicine, working with real-world medical data and deep learning models.

Ismail combines strong technical skills with a structured and analytical mindset. He is highly motivated, quick to learn, and able to adapt rapidly to new technologies and environments. He enjoys working both independently and in collaborative teams.

## Professional Focus

Ismail is particularly interested in:

- Machine learning and deep learning systems
- Retrieval-based AI systems (RAG)
- Software development
- Backend and full-stack development
- Data engineering and data-driven applications

## Personal Qualities
